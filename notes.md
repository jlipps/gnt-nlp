Transliteration
-----

Marks

*  *: capital
*  ): smooth breathing (after lowercase vowel or before uppercase vowel)
*  /: acute (after lowercase vowel or before uppercase vowel)
*  \: grave (after lowercase vowel or before uppercase vowel)
*  =: circumflex
*  (: rough breathing
*  ': elision
*  +: umlaut
*  |: iota subscript

Letters
*  A: alpha
*  B: beta
*  G: gamma
*  D: delta
*  E: epsilon
*  F: phi
*  H: eta
*  I: iota
*  K: kappa
*  L: lambda
*  M: mu
*  N: nu
*  O: omicron
*  P: pi
*  R: rho
*  C: xi
*  S: sigma
*  T: tau
*  U: upsilon
*  Y: psi
*  Q: theta
*  X: chi
*  Z: zeta
*  W: omega
